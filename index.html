Jeopardy, the name of a popular gameshow, derives from this old french term meaning "an even match"

After clicking the start button below you will select your trivia category. Once you've selected your category, the questions will appear on your screen one after another. You will have 15 seconds to answer each question by selecting one of the options below the question. After you answer or if you run out of time the quiz will move on to the next question. At the end of the category you will see how many questions you guessed correctly and how many you got wrong or for which you ran out of time.
